Our escrow system ensures both freelancers and clients feel protected. When a client approves a project, they deposit the agreed-upon amount into escrow. Once you complete your work and the client approves the deliverables, we release the payment directly to your account. If there's any dispute, our team reviews the evidence and makes a fair decision. This means you never have to worry about non-payment.

Verification typically takes 24-48 hours. Our team carefully reviews your profile, portfolio, and credentials to ensure you're a legitimate professional. Once verified, you'll receive a badge that significantly increases client trust and your chances of winning projects. The verification process is simple and straightforward—just upload your documents and let us do the work.

Disputes are rare on FreelanceFlow, but we have a fair resolution process in place. Our experienced mediators review all evidence from both parties—including communication, deliverables, and project agreements. We prioritize finding a win-win solution. In most cases, disputes are resolved within 48-72 hours. Our goal is to protect both professionals and clients while maintaining the integrity of our platform.
